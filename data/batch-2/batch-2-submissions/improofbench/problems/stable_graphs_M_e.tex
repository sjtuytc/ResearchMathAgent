\textbf{Problem.} Given $e \geq 0$ and $g \geq 2$, denote by $M_e(g)$
the number of isomorphism classes of stable graphs of genus $g$ with
$n = 0$ legs and precisely $e$ edges. For fixed $e$, analyze the
structure of the function
\[
M_e(-) : \{\, g \in \mathbb{Z} : g \geq 2 \,\} \longrightarrow
\mathbb{Z}_{\geq 0}, \qquad g \longmapsto M_e(g),
\]
and prove a structure result. Give a complete, self-contained
description of $M_e(-)$ for $e = 0, 1, 2, 3$.

\bigskip

\noindent\textbf{Sample solution sketch (for human validation only;
the agent must produce its own argument).} The function $M_e(g)$ is
eventually (for $g$ sufficiently large, depending on $e$) a
quasi-polynomial in $g$. For example, for $e = 3$ one has $M_3(2) = 2$
and, for $g \geq 3$,
\[
M_3(g) = \begin{cases}
\dfrac{1}{9} g^3 + \dfrac{7}{8} g^2 + \dfrac{5}{12} g - 2
   & \text{if } g \equiv 0 \pmod 6, \\[6pt]
\dfrac{1}{9} g^3 + \dfrac{7}{8} g^2 + \dfrac{1}{6} g - \dfrac{155}{72}
   & \text{if } g \equiv 1 \pmod 6, \\[6pt]
\dfrac{1}{9} g^3 + \dfrac{7}{8} g^2 + \dfrac{5}{12} g - \dfrac{20}{9}
   & \text{if } g \equiv 2 \pmod 6, \\[6pt]
\dfrac{1}{9} g^3 + \dfrac{7}{8} g^2 + \dfrac{1}{6} g - \dfrac{19}{8}
   & \text{if } g \equiv 3 \pmod 6, \\[6pt]
\dfrac{1}{9} g^3 + \dfrac{7}{8} g^2 + \dfrac{5}{12} g - \dfrac{16}{9}
   & \text{if } g \equiv 4 \pmod 6, \\[6pt]
\dfrac{1}{9} g^3 + \dfrac{7}{8} g^2 + \dfrac{1}{6} g - \dfrac{187}{72}
   & \text{if } g \equiv 5 \pmod 6.
\end{cases}
\]
